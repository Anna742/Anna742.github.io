Who I amThe storyteller

My name is Anna Sherwin. I am a college student looking to stretch myself as a creator and artist. I have delved into many forms of creative design, but storytelling has become my favorite.

I have loved telling stories for as long as I can remember. This desire grew in high school as I learned more about creative writing and photography. My two favorites are documentary films and short written stories.
